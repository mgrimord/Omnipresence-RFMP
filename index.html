Omnipresence


amplifier of personal presence


a Room for More Possibilities Prototype





Communication across space and time is a form of mobility. Car is a form of communication with the future. Omnipresence gives us the ability to make an emotive connection with the future, sending and receiving touch signals, amplifying a distant presence of the Infiniti driver.





1

Driver grips the wheel to send a signal to the future.

2

At the future destination, those waiting see the light orb pulse.

3

They touch the orb to send a signal back to the traveler.

4

Traveler receives a haptic message.
